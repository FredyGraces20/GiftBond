‚ùì COMMON QUESTIONS:

Q: Do players need permissions?
A: By default, giftbond.use is given to everyone. Customize in config.

Q: How do I add custom gifts?
A: Edit /plugins/GiftBond/gifts.yml - auto-generated with examples.

Q: Can I disable playtime requirements?
A: Yes! Set hours_requirement.enabled: false in config.yml.

Q: How does the dynamic points system work?
A: Points are calculated when gifts are CLAIMED based on the sender's current boost level.

Q: Is it compatible with my existing economy plugin?
A: Yes! GiftBond works with Vault-compatible economy plugins.
